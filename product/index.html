EIL
Product: Introduction

Objective

  • To provide insights for the newly joined sales manager on the APAC’s EIL product and processes.
  • To maximize the productivity of the sales manager from day one.
  • To make sales manager understand the end to end processes ie; from sourcing to post disbursement monitoring.
  • To make the sales manager understand the APAC’s code of conduct.

Introduction

  • APAC’s EIL product offers finance to the below segment:
    • Schools recognized by Board of Education in respective state /CBSE/ICSE/IB/IG recognized or any other recognized affiliation schools.
    • Established & recognized Play group with decent track record of students getting admission in established schools.

Purpose

  • To cover Construction cost of new/additional building.
  • Carrying repairs/alterations/additions to the existing building.
  • Purchase of land for School playground or for new school building (existing school expanding to new building).
  • Expansion of School by adding new branch.
  • Purchase of infrastructure such as furniture, benches, desks, smart class, computers, labs equipments, etc.
  • Repayment of current high cost loan and consolidation of debt.
  • Upgrade or creation of educational infrastructure facilities such as auditorium, activity room, or any other facility.

What is K12 Educaton?

  • The K-12 education is the term used to denote the education imparted in the primary and secondary phases of school life, including K or kindergarten to 12 which stands for the 12th standard.
  • This system covers education from kindergarten till the 12th grade.

Why our focus on K12 Sector?

K-12 education is highly regulated at Central and state government levels in India
Regulatory framework as per the affiliated board (CBSE, ICSE, IB, etc.)
Lower Delinquency in this sector as seen by other lenders

APAC
Program Lending for Educational Institutions

Target Market K12 Schools
Industry Education Industry
Loan Amount Rs. 25 Lakhs to 300 Lakhs
Loan Tenure Upto 84 months, Avg. Tensure 5 years
Interest Rate 16% to 18% p.a.
Processing Fee 1% to 2% of loan amount
LTV
  • School Property- Upto 45% of Market Value of the Property
  • Residential- Upto 70% of Market Value of the Property
  • Commercial- Upto 60% of Market Value of the Property

(All the properties shall be of Non Agri in nature and Vacant commercial property is not accepted)
