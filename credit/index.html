Credit and Risk Module

Loan Process

Origination/ Pre-Login

The Sales Manager shall identify the Educational Institute
Schedule a Sales meeting
Understand the financial requirement of the Institution

During Sales visit to the clients location :

Collect the duly filled Loan application form from the client
Prepare a sales visit report
Collect all the documents as per requirement list

Documents Required:

Application form Duly filled with photographs & signature.

Sales Visit Login Report.

KYC documents as mentioned above of all Co-applicants with OSV by Sales Manager/officer.

Bank statement of Educational Institute , Trust /Society and that of individuals for last 12 months.

Constitution documents such as Trust Deed, MOA/AOA,Society Bye laws and the latest amended trust deeds if any.

Audited Financial & ITR.

Sanction letter of all existing loans along with loan track availed by Entity and as well as individuals.

Student strength and fee particulars on the society letter head.

Property documents copies.

Key points to adhere in sales visit:

Adherence to APAC’s code of conduct.
Shall understand the client’s requirement.
Shall build rapport with the client.
Shall observe the school premises and students.
Shall explain the client our product and our procedures.

Fair Practice Code

No Cash Accepted
Acknowledgment of loan application form to be given back to clients.
The Red Carpet treatment to clients!
Transparency in charges and Fees.

KYC Policy

Individual
  • PAN/ Form 60
  • Aadhar Card (Redact or blackout Aadhaar number from hard copy obtained)
  • Valid passport
  • Voter’s Identity card
  • Valid driving license
Companies
  • Certificate of Incorporation and Memorandum and Articles of Association
  • PAN
  • List of latest shareholding patterns and latest directors list
  • KYC docs for Individual Signatory/ POA Holder or Beneficial Owner
  • Resolution of the Board of Directors for Authorised Signatory
Patnership Firm
  • Registration Certificate
  • Partnership deed
  • PAN
  • KYC docs for Individual Partner as Signatory/POA Holder or Beneficial Owner
  • Telephone bill in the name of firm and/or partners
  • Power of Attorney granted to a partner or an employee of the firm to transact business on its behalf
  • Any officially valid document identifying the partners and the persons holding the Power of Attorney and their addresses
Trust & Foundations
  • PAN/ Form 60
  • Certificate of Registration
  • Trust Deed
  • Resolution of the managing body of the foundation/ association
  • Power of attorney granted to transact business of its behalf
Sole Proprietary Firm
  • Registration Certificate
  • PAN
  • Certificate/licence issued by the municipal authorities under Shop and Establishment Act.
  • Complete Income Tax Return in the name of Proprietary Firm
  • GST Certificate (Provisional or Final)
Unincorporated Association
  • PAN
  • Resolution of the managing body of such association
  • Power of attorney granted to him to transact on its behalf
  • An officially valid document in respect of the person holding an attorney to transact on its behalf
Eligible FPIs under PIS
  • PAN
  • Proof of address
  • SEBI registration certificate
  • Senior management list
  • Authorised signatories list
  • Constitutive documents

Further Process:

Credit Team shall carry the CIBIL checks and if CIBIL check is positive then the Sales Manager shall then schedule a PD for the client else the proposal would be dropped.
Credit manager will understand the background, history of institution, cashflow, Bank account, constitution of society, Student strength verification, attendance register, fee receipts etc in order to prepare CAM
After PD, nominal fee of Rs. 10,000 for Loan upto Rs. 1 cr and Rs. 20,000 for Loan more than 1cr is to be collected towards legal & valuation expenses
Credit Manager will then prepare CAM, and will parallelly initiate Legal, Technical, FI & RCU with empanelled vendors of APAC

Credit Manager to focus on:

Income assessment is the most critical & needs to be captured in CAM
Required reference checks to be done
Existing Track record and Eligibility Criteria
CAM will be decisioned as per the delegation matrix and will put up to the approving authority
Generate the sanction letter once the case is approved

Sanction Letter

Sales Manager must get it signed by applicant & co-applicants for acceptance of sanction
One copy will be given to the Client
One copy will be kept in the loan file
